= BPI-2K3000 industrial computer

BPI-2K3000 is industrial Mini PC developed by Banana Pi open source community with Loongson 2K3000 processor.

BPI-2K3000 can be applied to enterprise office, personal minicomputs, high-performance industrial computer, as well as education, medical, finance, edge computing and other fields. It has the advantages of high performance computing, portability, low energy consumption and easy management.
