# BpiRouter OS —  Recovery and unbricking


> BpiRouter OS Core reliability capabilities: Form「error correction configuration」to「whole device becomes a brick」，There are rescue measures at each level. **If it's flashed incorrectly, it can still be rescued and won't become a permanent brick.**

## 0. First, identify your [Restore button]

Different boards use different keys for recovery.（This point is very crucial.）：

[options="header",cols="2,4"]
|====
| **Board** | **Reset button** 
| **BPI-R4** | **WPS button**（The reset key of R4 is a hard reset line and is invisible to software, so use WPS for recovery.） 
| **BPI-R4 Lite** | Physics  **reset** botton
| **BPI-R4 Pro** | Physics  **reset** botton
| **BPI-R3** | Physics  **reset** botton
| **BPI-R3 Mini** | Physics **reset** botton

|====

> The "reset button" mentioned below corresponds =  the one on your board.

## 1.  Four-layer recovery ladder

From the lightest (not losing anything) to the heaviest (resetting the whole device to factory settings), provide support at each level. **Usually, you'll only use L3.**

### L1 · Configure automatic rollback (automatic，no operation required)
When you modify network / WiFi / Firewall settings that may cut off the management connection, a message will appear **confirmation banner appears  + confirmation banner appears ** at the top of the interface：

- The changes are normal. You can still access it. → Click [Save Changes] to make it take effect;
- Correcting mistakes led to disconnection. You didn't click confirm. → **Automatically resume when the countdown ends ** return the configuration before the change.

This layer of protection is enabled by default, ensuring that [even if you make a mistake, you won't lock yourself out].

### L2 · Firmware automatic rollbac（automatic，no operation required）

Firmware upgrade uses A/B dual slots: If the new firmware fails to start,**The bootloader will automatically switch back to the previous working firmware slot.**.You don't need to perform any operations. The device will automatically return to a usable version.

### L3 · Restore to factory settings（Press and hold the recovery key for about 5 seconds during operation.）

When the device can run normally, but the **configuration is messed up / the password is forgotten**:

1. When the device is in operation.，**Press and hold the recovery key for about 5 seconds** and then release it.
2. The device clears the current configuration, restarts and returns to the factory state.（**Do not reflash the firmware**）.
3. Reopen `https://192.168.100.1` and do [link:/en/BpiRouterOS/BananaPi_BpiRouterOS_Setup[Configuration settings]]。

> Only clear the configuration of the current firmware slot, leave the other slot unchanged, do not and re-flash the firmware.

### L4 · Full device recovery（Press and hold the recovery key to power on after the power is cut off.）

when **Both the configuration and the firmware are damaged. / The device fails to start up** ultimate fallback：

1. **Power off.**
2. **Keep pressing the reset button.**， power on the device. **Keep pressing throughout the whole process**。
3. The device restores the whole machine to the factory settings from the built-in golden image.（When connecting to the serial port, you can see prompts like **GOLDENORE REST**.）。
4. After completion, it will automatically restart and to return the pure factory state.

> ⚠️ **You need to keep pressing to get past the self-check/countdown during startup.**，Release your finger only when you see the prompt indicating that the device recovery process has started. Releasing it too early will be regarded as a normal startup.

> 💡 R4-Lite with independent NOR+NAND ，Before rescuing the bricked device, you may need to switch the NAND/NOR DIP switch to Nand on the board .——Please refer to the factory programming instructions that come with the firmware for details.

## 2. How to choose which layer?

[options="header",cols="5,6"]
|====
| **Situation** | **Which layer to use** 
| It can't connect after I changed a setting.| L1（Wait for the countdown to automatically roll back, or manually roll back the next time you enter.） 
| There are problems with the new firmware after the upgrade. | L2 automatic rollback ；Or click [Roll back to the previous version] on the system page.
| Forgot the management password / The configuration is in a mess, but the device can still be powered on. | **L3**：Press and hold the reset key for 5 seconds during operation.
| Fails to start up / device is bricked / device restarts repeatedly | **L4**：Power off→Press and hold the reset key→Power on
|====

## 3. Common problems

**There is no response when long - pressing the reset key?（R4）**

The reset key of BPI-R4 is a hard reset key, and the software cannot read it.——**The recovery key of BPI-R4 is the WPS key**，please use WPS botton。other board use physics reset botton。

**L4 Pressed botton but didn't enter the brick rescue mode?**

Most likely, you released your finger too early.Be sure to keep pressing and skip the boot countdown until you see the prompt for rescuing a bricked device.

**Will rescuing a bricked device cause the loss of configurations?**

Yes——L4 It will return to the pure factory state. The risk of losing daily configurations is covered by L1 (automatic rollback) and [retain configurations during upgrade]；L4 is the last resort。It is recommended to use the [Download Backup] function on the system page to save a copy of the configuration. You can upload it after restoration to revert to the previous settings.
